Welcome to the Underground

Black Rabbit is Austin's hidden gem for live comedy—a speakeasy-style club tucked beneath the streets of downtown. Step through the golden door and descend into an intimate, 50-seat room where the city's best comedians perform just feet away from you.

We're proudly BYOB, so grab your drinks from Hot Spot Liquor Store next door and settle in for a night of laughs in our moody, underground atmosphere.
